Tutorial: A catalog homepage

In this tutorial, we will build a homepage for an Open Data portal.

Datasets are usually gathered in catalogs, which are collections organized using the various metadata of the datasets; for example, an Open Data portal is a catalog of datasets. Of course, that doesn't look very sexy in itself, and it is often a good idea to present a nice preview of a catalog to engage visitors to explore it further. We have several widgets that will help us to achieve that.

First, let's create a new catalog context plugged on OpenDataSoft's public portal.

<ods-catalog-context context="catalog" catalog-domain="public.opendatasoft.com"></ods-catalog-context>

We want to write a nice welcome sentence that contains the number of datasets available, to make it look alive. We have a widget that can help us to this: odsDomainStatistics exposes a number of statistics about the domain of the context it is declared on, as variables in our page; for example, we can obtain a catalog.stats.dataset variable containing the number of datasets.

<ods-catalog-context context="catalog" catalog-domain="public.opendatasoft.com" ods-domain-statistics>
    Welcome on our public portal! Take a tour of our {{catalog.stats.dataset}} available datasets!
</ods-catalog-context>
Welcome on our public portal! Take a tour of our {{catalog.stats.dataset}} available datasets!

A simple number doesn't say much: what are most datasets talking about? What are the most recent datasets and the most popular ones? We have a number of widgets that can answer these questions in a simple manner: odsTagCloud, odsLastDatasetsFeed, and odsMostPopularDatasets. We just need to tell our tag cloud widget which metadata to use (here, we need the keywords).

<ods-catalog-context context="catalog" catalog-domain="public.opendatasoft.com" ods-domain-statistics>
    <h2>Welcome on our public portal! Take a tour of our {{catalog.stats.dataset}} available datasets!</h2>
    <div class="row-fluid">
        <div class="span6">
            <ods-tag-cloud facet-name="keyword" context="catalog"></ods-tag-cloud>
        </div>
        <div class="span6">
            <h3>Last datasets</h3>
            <ods-last-datasets-feed context="catalog"></ods-last-datasets-feed>
            <h3>Most popular</h3>
            <ods-most-popular-datasets context="catalog"></ods-most-popular-datasets>
            <ods-last-reuses-feed context="catalog"></ods-last-reuses-feed>
        </div>
    </div>
</ods-catalog-context>
